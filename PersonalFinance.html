Personal Finance/Career Work Experience

Personal Finance

1 Semester/0.5 Credit

Grades 10 11 12

CTE Dual Credit

Taught by: FILL IN


Course Description: This course will help students manage their finance in life after high school. Topics of study will include but are not limited to:

  • Money management
  • Creating a budget
  • Checkbook use and reconciliation
  • Credit cards
  • Loans
  • Planning for retirement
  • Investing
  • Automobile purchasing and ownership
  • Apartment living

This course may be applied toward the Business and Marketing and Family and Consumer Sciences certificates. There will not be much homework as most work is completed during class.



Career Work Experience

1 Semester/0.5 Credit or 2 Semesters/1.0 Credit

Grades 10 11 12

Taught by: FILL IN


Course Description: Students who are taking or have taken an occupational course are eligible to register for credit for work experience. Students must complete a minimum of 180 hours of work for each .50 credit awarded, and must also sign a training agreement as well as participate in a formal evaluation process. This course may be applied toward the Business and Marketing Certificate.
